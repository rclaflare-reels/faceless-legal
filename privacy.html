Privacy Policy

Faceless Reels · Effective 2026-04-14

1. Summary

Faceless Reels ("the App") is a personal, single-user tool. It does not collect, store, or share personal information from any user other than its operator (the "Operator"). This policy describes what data the App handles and why.

2. Data the App Handles

The App processes the following on the Operator's local machine:

  • OAuth tokens issued by TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram for the Operator's own accounts. Tokens are stored locally and used only to publish videos the Operator has produced.
  • Video files produced locally by the Operator, which are uploaded to the Operator's own accounts on the destination platforms.
  • Basic account metadata (such as the authenticated account's username or ID) returned by each platform's API, used solely to confirm that uploads are being directed to the correct account.

3. Data the App Does Not Collect

  • The App does not collect information about other users of TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram.
  • The App has no server component, no analytics, no telemetry, and no advertising.
  • The App does not share any data with third parties beyond the platform APIs required to fulfill upload requests.

4. Data Storage and Retention

All tokens and credentials reside on the Operator's local device. They can be revoked at any time by the Operator in the respective platform's account settings, and deleted locally by removing the relevant credential files.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 13 and does not knowingly process any data from children.
